The Great Gatsby

Author: F. Scott Fitzgerald

Published: 1925

Genre: Fiction, Classic

My Review

"The Great Gatsby" is a timeless classic that explores themes of love, wealth, and the American Dream. F. Scott Fitzgerald's writing is both poetic and critical, offering a vivid portrayal of the Jazz Age. The characters, especially Jay Gatsby, are complex and memorable. While the story is set in the 1920s, its themes remain relevant today. The novel's tragic ending leaves a lasting impression, making it a must-read.

Overall, I highly recommend this book to anyone who enjoys thought-provoking literature. It's a masterpiece that deserves its place in the literary canon.
